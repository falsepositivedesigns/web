About False +

False + is an independent design initiative pushing the boundaries of privacy-first fashion and digital art works. In a world where every camera is watching, we create apparel, accessories, and visual art meant to disrupt facial recognition and tracking systems.

Our team of creators, technologists, and privacy advocates believes digital surveillance has exceeded ethical boundaries. We strive to put self-defense back into the hands of individuals by turning every garment and art piece into a quiet act of resistance.

Who We Design For

From clothing to adversarial art works, our collections are built for developers, security researchers, activists, and anyone questioning the spread of surveillance capitalism. Every piece combines technical craft, art direction, and cyberpunk attitude—made to help you make a statement visually and ideologically.

The Future of Digital Self-Defense

False + is committed to ongoing research at the intersection of art, technology, and privacy. As threats evolve and AI becomes ever more invasive, so do the tools for resistance. While no solution is 100% foolproof, we believe that awareness and action—expressed even through what you wear or display—can shift the balance.
