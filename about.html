About False Positive

False Positive is an independent design initiative pushing the boundaries of privacy-first fashion. In a world where every camera is watching, we create apparel and accessories aimed at disrupting facial recognition and tracking systems.

Our team of creators, technologists, and privacy advocates believes that digital surveillance has exceeded ethical boundaries. We strive to put self-defense back into the hands of individuals by turning every garment into a quiet act of resistance.

Who We Design For

Our collections are built for developers, security researchers, activists, and anyone questioning the spread of surveillance capitalism. Every piece combines technical craft with cyberpunk attitude to let you make a statement—visually and ideologically.

The Future of Digital Self-Defense

False Positive is committed to ongoing research at the intersection of art, technology, and privacy. As threats evolve and AI becomes ever more invasive, so do the tools for resistance. While no solution is 100% foolproof, we believe that awareness and action—expressed even through what you wear—can shift the balance.
